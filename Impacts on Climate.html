Power BI - The Impact of Human Actions on Climate

Participation in the Power BI DataViz World Championships held by Microsoft.

I had one week to gather the necessary information, perform the transformations, format the data, and understand the entire dataset.
After creating the DAX measures and refining the model, I started building the visuals. My first version was a dashboard designed for frequent monitoring of the indicators. However, the competition's focus was more on the visuals and the story told through the data.
After researching various models, I chose a unique structure resembling a brochure. The result is just below.
